The Problem

Brand analytics is fundamentally broken

Brands spend billions on analytics tools that tell them what happened. Nobody tells them what their customers actually experience — before the purchase decision is made.

01

SEO tools lie by omission

They show keyword rankings — not whether a real shopper discovers you during a genuine purchase journey starting from a lifestyle search.

02

Ad platforms grade their own homework

Google and Meta report impressions, not whether the ad reached the right psychological profile at the right moment of intent.

03

Pricing data misses the experience

Competitor prices are scraped in bulk — not experienced the way a consumer actually encounters and judges them side-by-side.
