How It Works

Agents live as consumers, not shoppers

1

Define the consumer, not the product

Personas have lifestyle interests, not search terms. "Style-conscious 30-year-old furnishing a first apartment."

2

Agents start from inspiration, not intent

Each agent begins with a lifestyle search — "futuristic room inspiration" — not a product query. Purchase intent emerges naturally.

3

Journeys run to completion

Agents continue across Google, Reddit, Pinterest, Amazon until they purchase or give up. Every decision is logged in consumer voice.

4

Scale delivers confidence

30+ agents per run. 100 agents is reportable. Every finding links to a real agent transcript.
